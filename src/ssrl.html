my work as a web app developer

for the UGA Small Satellite Research Laboratory

During fall of 2024, I joined the Lab Operations team as a web developer. This team maintains a central position, working with all departments and missions within the lab. This gave me the opportunity to collaborate with people of different disciplines and backgrounds.

I was lucky to be able to join at a time where I would get to play a vital role from the start. At the time the lab was brainstorming launch a new mission called LEARNSat wherein we would collaborate with local high school students to host competitions and get students involved with STEM using satellites. With a motive to generate revenue for the lab, we planned to sell these "cubesat kits" paired with online learning modules to allow students to participate and learn at their own pace.



So in addition to my responsibilities of maintaining and improving our current website's functionality and features, I took on a role of collaborating with the New Media Institute on the creation of the new "LEARNSat" site.

Spring 2025

In spring 2025 the team is continuing working on the content for the learning modules.

As the workload has increased I just onboarded with two additional web developers who will be working under me to continue SSRL after I graduate this May.
